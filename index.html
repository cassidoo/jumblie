How to play Jumblie

There are exactly four words hidden amongst the letters, and all letters are used exactly once. The words will always be between 4 and 9 letters long.

Click on the letters (or type them on your keyboard) to spell a word. When you have a word you want to submit, click the "Submit" button. If the word is one of the daily words, it will be added to your found list of words. Find all four words as fast as you can!
